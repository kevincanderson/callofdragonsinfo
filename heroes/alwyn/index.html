Master of Poisons

Epic

Alwyn

Magic | PvP | Control

G1

A reclusive botanist and former member of the Vale Society, skilled in the art of poisons.

Alwyn excels at controlling the battlefield with his skills and talents. Alwyn makes his presense known early in the free-to-play realm, but will dwindle away the farther the game goes one. He can be used in a third or forth march later in other seasons, but will be of good value in Season 1 and 2.

Skills

Venomous Overgrowth

Rage Skill

Deals single-target Magic damage and inflicts Ensnare.

Wealdshield

Passive Skill

Magic units gain Magic ATK and HP.

Oath of the Wellspring

Passive Skill

Reduces normal attack damage taken in the field.

Friend of the Hemlock

Passive Skill

Has a chance to inflict Ensnare upon the attacker when hit with a normal attack.

Miasmic Mist

Skill Enhancement: Venomous Growth

Deals single-target Magic damage and inflicts Ensnare. Deals extra Magic damage to targets with other Magic damage-dealing effects.
