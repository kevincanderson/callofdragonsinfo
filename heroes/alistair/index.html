Alistair

Cavalry | Rally | Tank

Epic

G1

Alistair is a early game hero who has some relavance later in the game, depending on how you are playing you account. Alistair can play well as a deputy early in the game, as his kit doesn't play into cavalry until he is awakened. He can pair well with commaders like Bakhar early as a deputy. If you plan to main cavalry on your account, Alistair will play a part for many seasons. Don't sleep on this guy!
